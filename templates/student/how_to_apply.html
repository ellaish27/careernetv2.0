PUJAB Application Guide

The Public Universities Joint Admissions Board (PUJAB) handles placement for government-sponsored students.

1
Acquire the Form

Purchase the PUJAB application form from designated banks (usually Stanbic or Centenary) after UACE results are released.

2
Fill Personal Details

Enter your Index Number, Gender, and District of Origin accurately.

3
Course Selection

Select your desired courses in order of priority. Ensure your subjects match the requirements (see Weighting System).

4
Submit

Submit the form to the Admission Board or apply online via the PUJAB portal before the deadline.

Advice: Always apply for a course where your calculated weight exceeds the previous year's cut-off point.
